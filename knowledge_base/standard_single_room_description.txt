The Standard Single Room is designed for solo travelers and includes one single bed, a work desk, blackout curtains, and a compact wardrobe. The room layout supports short business stays with ergonomic seating, bedside charging ports, and a wall-mounted smart TV.

Bathroom amenities include a walk-in shower, hairdryer, and eco-friendly toiletries. Average room size is 18 square meters. Complimentary WiFi, two bottles of water per day, and tea-and-coffee sachets are included. Maximum occupancy is one adult, with no rollaway bed option for this category.
