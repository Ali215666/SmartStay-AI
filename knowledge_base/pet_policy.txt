Pet-friendly rooms are limited and must be confirmed before arrival. Only domesticated cats and dogs within defined weight limits are permitted. Vaccination records and signed pet liability form are required at check-in.

Pets must remain leashed in public areas and cannot be left unattended in rooms without prior arrangement. Additional cleaning fees apply based on stay duration and room condition. Pets are not allowed in pool, gym, or main restaurant zones, except certified service animals under applicable law.
