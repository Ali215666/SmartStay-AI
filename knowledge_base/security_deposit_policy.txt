An incidental security deposit is collected or pre-authorized at check-in to cover minibar usage, damage, and unpaid auxiliary services. Deposit value varies by room category and length of stay and is communicated in booking confirmation terms.

Unused deposit balance is released after checkout audit. Release timelines depend on payment method and issuing bank processing cycles. Guests paying cash deposit receive documented receipt and must return it at checkout for settlement. Disputed deductions are reviewed by duty manager with housekeeping and engineering reports.
