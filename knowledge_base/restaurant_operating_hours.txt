The all-day dining restaurant operates from 12:00 PM to 10:30 PM for lunch and dinner service. Afternoon tea menu is available from 3:30 PM to 5:30 PM. Bar service follows separate licensing hours and may close earlier on weekdays.

Kitchen accepts final hot-food orders 30 minutes before closing. During public holidays or private events, operating windows may be adjusted with advance notice posted in lobby and guest portal. Reservations are recommended for groups of six or more to reduce waiting time.
