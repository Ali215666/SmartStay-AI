Early check-in is subject to room readiness and forecast occupancy. Guests arriving before 12:00 PM may request priority cleaning; however, immediate room access cannot be guaranteed. Requests are queued by arrival time, booking class, and loyalty status.

When early room access is approved before 10:00 AM, a half-day charge may apply unless waived through membership benefits or negotiated corporate terms. Guests may store luggage and use lobby facilities while waiting. Early check-in decisions are finalized on the arrival date and cannot be guaranteed at booking stage.
