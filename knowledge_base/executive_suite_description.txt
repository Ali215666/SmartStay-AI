The Executive Suite includes a separate bedroom and living area, providing privacy for business meetings or extended stays. Standard features include one king bed, sofa seating, dining table for two, and dual smart TVs in bedroom and lounge zones.

Suite bathrooms include a bathtub plus separate shower, plush towels, and upgraded toiletries. Average suite size is 52 square meters. Guests also receive priority check-in queue and late check-out consideration based on occupancy. This category supports up to two adults and one child with optional rollaway bed.
