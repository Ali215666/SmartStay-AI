Registered guests may receive visitors in lobby and designated public areas. In-room visitors must be declared at front desk with valid identification and are subject to local security regulations and occupancy limits.

Visitor access is generally permitted between 8:00 AM and 10:00 PM unless management grants written exception. Overnight stays by undeclared visitors are prohibited and may trigger additional guest charges or booking termination. Repeated violations are escalated to security and duty manager.
