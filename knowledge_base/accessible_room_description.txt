The Accessible Room is configured for guests with mobility needs and complies with inclusive design standards. Features include widened doorways, step-free entry, lowered switches, grab bars, roll-in shower, and emergency assistance pull cords.

The room includes one queen bed, clear turning radius for wheelchairs, and reachable wardrobe rail height. Hearing-assistance and visual-alert devices are available upon request. Companion occupancy is permitted within category limits. Guests are encouraged to specify support requirements in advance so equipment can be pre-installed before arrival.
