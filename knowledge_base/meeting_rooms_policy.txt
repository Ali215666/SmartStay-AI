Meeting rooms are available in boardroom, classroom, and U-shape configurations with capacity guidelines defined per room. Bookings include standard projector, screen, and WiFi. Additional AV equipment should be requested at least 12 hours in advance.

Room occupancy limits must be observed for fire safety compliance. Catering, stationery kits, and breakout setup are billed according to selected package. Time overruns are charged in hourly increments after a 15-minute grace period. Final attendee count should be confirmed one day prior.
