Group bookings apply to reservations of 10 rooms or more on overlapping dates. A rooming list with guest names, occupancy, and arrival schedules must be submitted at least 7 days before check-in. Unsold rooms in the block are released based on the contract's cut-off date.

Group rates include agreed inclusions only; additional services are billed separately unless specified in writing. A designated group coordinator must handle all amendments and billing instructions. Last-minute room type upgrades, extra beds, and early arrivals are accommodated based on inventory and may trigger supplemental charges.
