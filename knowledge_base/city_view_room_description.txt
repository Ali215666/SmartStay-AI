The City View Room overlooks the central business and shopping district, offering elevated skyline perspective, especially during evening hours. Layout includes one queen or king bed, compact sitting area, and desk for remote work needs.

Typical area is 26 square meters with sound-insulated windows to reduce street noise. Amenities include smart TV, minibar, in-room safe, and complimentary high-speed WiFi. Guests requesting quieter sleep conditions may ask for higher floors and non-connecting room allocation during pre-arrival planning.
