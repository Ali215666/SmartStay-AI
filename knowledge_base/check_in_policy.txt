Standard check-in time is 3:00 PM. Front desk is staffed 24 hours, and guests may arrive earlier to complete registration. Room keys are issued once the assigned room has passed final inspection. During high occupancy periods, priority is given to guests with confirmed guaranteed bookings and completed pre-arrival forms.

Guests must present a valid government-issued photo ID and a booking confirmation number at check-in. A pre-authorization hold for incidentals is required unless waived under an approved corporate account. Requests for floor preference, connecting rooms, or specific bedding are noted at arrival and fulfilled based on availability.
