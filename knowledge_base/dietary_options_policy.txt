The kitchen supports vegetarian, vegan, halal-compliant, and low-allergen meal options. Guests should communicate dietary requirements at booking stage or at least four hours before meal service for best preparation quality.

Cross-contact controls are in place; however, a shared kitchen environment may not guarantee zero allergen exposure. For severe allergies, guests must speak with duty chef before ordering. Ingredient lists and substitution options are available on request, and halal sourcing follows approved vendor certifications.
