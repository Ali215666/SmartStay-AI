All adult guests must present valid government-issued photo identification at check-in. Passport is required for foreign nationals, and local regulations may require digital registration with authorities. Name on booking should match the primary guest's ID.

Photocopies or expired documents are not accepted unless approved by legal compliance desk in exceptional circumstances. For corporate bookings, traveler identity still requires physical verification. Refusal to provide valid ID may result in denied check-in without refund under legal and security obligations.
