Advance payment requirements depend on rate type and season. Flexible rates typically require card guarantee only, while discounted and event-period rates require full or partial prepayment at booking. Payment links remain valid for 24 hours unless otherwise stated in the offer terms.

Bookings not funded within the payment window are automatically cancelled by the reservation system. For international cards, currency conversion and issuer fees are outside hotel control. Receipts for advance payments are issued electronically and reflected in the guest folio at check-in.
