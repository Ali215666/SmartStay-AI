Room pricing follows seasonal demand calendars, city events, and occupancy forecasts. Peak season rates are activated for holidays, festivals, and major conventions, while low-season rates include value-add packages such as breakfast credit or extended-stay discounts.

Published rates may change daily until reservation confirmation is completed. Existing confirmed bookings remain protected under booked terms unless the guest modifies dates or room type. Corporate and negotiated rates are governed by contract validity periods and blackout dates defined in the commercial agreement.
