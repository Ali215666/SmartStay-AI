The hotel is located in the central district, approximately 35 minutes from the international airport under normal traffic conditions. Main access roads include Riverside Avenue and Central Business Boulevard, with clear signage at the final junction.

Guests arriving by metro should exit at City Center Station Gate 3 and walk about 8 minutes. Ride-share and taxi drop-off is available at the main entrance canopy. For self-driving guests, parking entry is on the east side lane behind the lobby block.
