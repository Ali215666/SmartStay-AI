The Sea View Room offers direct or angled ocean-facing windows with seating positioned to maximize natural light. Room configuration includes one king bed or two twins, writing desk, blackout curtains, and balcony access where architecturally available.

Average area is 28 square meters. Weather conditions may affect visibility and balcony access advisories. This category includes standard room amenities plus binocular rental on request. Guests sensitive to wind noise may request interior corridor placement, though sea-facing guarantees are maintained for booked category.
