The Deluxe Room offers additional living space, upgraded bedding, and enhanced bathroom amenities compared to standard categories. Typical room size is 30 square meters, with either one king bed or two twin beds depending on inventory allocation.

Guests receive premium bath products, higher-floor priority where available, and expanded beverage setup. The room includes a lounge chair, larger work surface, and additional wardrobe storage. Deluxe inventory is recommended for stays longer than three nights or travelers requiring both work and relaxation space.
