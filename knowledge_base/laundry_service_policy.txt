Laundry collection is available daily between 8:00 AM and 8:00 PM. Same-day return is offered for items handed in before 10:00 AM; later submissions are returned the next day. Express service is available with additional fee.

Guests must complete the laundry slip and indicate preferred treatment type. The hotel is not liable for color bleed or damage caused by undeclared fabric sensitivity. Delicate garments and dry-clean-only items are processed according to care label instructions. Unclaimed laundry is held for seven days before storage transfer.
