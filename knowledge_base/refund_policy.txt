Eligible refunds are processed to the original payment method once the reservation is validated as refundable under rate conditions. Standard card refunds are initiated within 3 business days, while bank transfer refunds may require up to 10 business days depending on intermediary processing.

Refunds for shortened stays are calculated using the booked rate plan and actual consumed nights. Non-refundable deposits, service add-ons already consumed, and no-show penalties are not refundable. Guests disputing charges must submit a written claim within 7 days of departure with booking number, invoice copy, and payment reference.
