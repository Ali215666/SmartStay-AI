In-room dining orders are prepared in sequence of receipt and delivered to guest rooms by service staff only. Guests should confirm room number, dietary restrictions, and preferred cutlery setup while placing the order.

Food quality is guaranteed at delivery point; replacement requests should be reported within 15 minutes. Outside food handling by hotel staff is not permitted due to food safety compliance. Trays and service trolleys should not be left in hallways for extended periods due to hygiene and evacuation standards.
