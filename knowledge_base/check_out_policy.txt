Standard check-out time is 11:00 AM. Guests may complete check-out at the front desk, through the in-room TV menu, or via express check-out in the mobile guest portal. Luggage storage is available at the bell desk until midnight on the departure date.

Late check-out requests must be submitted before 9:00 AM on the day of departure. Approved extensions until 1:00 PM are complimentary for loyalty members at eligible tiers; extensions beyond 1:00 PM may incur an hourly charge. Unreturned key cards, minibar usage, and damages are added to the final folio before invoice closure.
