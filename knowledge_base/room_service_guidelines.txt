Room service is available from 6:30 AM to 11:00 PM, with a limited overnight menu from 11:00 PM to 6:00 AM. Orders can be placed through in-room phone extension or the guest mobile portal. Estimated delivery time is displayed at ordering stage.

For food safety and quality, orders should be consumed within 45 minutes of delivery. Tray collection requests are attended within 30 minutes. Service charges may apply depending on menu section and time band. Alcohol service follows local legal restrictions and identification verification rules.
