Individual reservations may be cancelled without penalty up to 48 hours before scheduled arrival. Cancellations received within 48 hours are subject to a one-night room-and-tax charge. Non-refundable promotional rates, event packages, and flash-sale bookings are excluded from flexible cancellation terms.

Cancellation requests must be submitted through the original booking channel or directly to reservations with the booking ID. A written cancellation acknowledgment is issued by email and should be retained for dispute resolution. Time calculations are based on the hotel's local time zone, not the guest's departure location.
