The Standard Double Room features one queen bed or two twin beds, subject to assignment at check-in. The room includes a writing desk, reading lights on both sides, and a luggage bench suitable for medium-stay guests.

Average area is 24 square meters with ensuite bathroom and shower enclosure. In-room amenities include smart TV, minibar, digital safe, and complimentary WiFi. Maximum occupancy is two adults and one child below six years sharing existing bedding. Twin-bed preference should be requested before arrival.
