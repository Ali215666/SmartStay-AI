The fitness center is open from 6:00 AM to 10:00 PM for registered guests aged 16 and above. Proper athletic footwear and workout attire are mandatory. Guests should sanitize equipment after each use and return accessories to designated racks.

Use of gym facilities is at guest's own risk, and medical conditions should be disclosed before intensive workouts. Personal training sessions are available by appointment. During peak hours, machine use may be time-limited to ensure fair access for all users.
