The hotel is a non-smoking property in all guestrooms and indoor public areas unless a designated smoking zone is clearly marked. Use of cigarettes, cigars, e-cigarettes, and vaping devices is restricted to approved outdoor points.

Any evidence of indoor smoking may result in deep-cleaning and odor-removal charges posted to guest folio. Tampering with smoke detectors is treated as a serious safety violation and may lead to immediate eviction. Guests should contact front desk for the nearest authorized smoking location.
