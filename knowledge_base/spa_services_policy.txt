Spa operates from 10:00 AM to 9:00 PM with treatment bookings in 30- to 90-minute slots. Guests are requested to arrive 15 minutes early for consultation and consent forms. Late arrival may shorten treatment duration to protect subsequent appointments.

Cancellations made within four hours of appointment may incur a fee. Certain treatments are not recommended for guests with specific medical conditions; pre-screening is required. Couples therapy rooms and premium packages are available subject to therapist roster and advance confirmation.
