The Presidential Suite is the hotel's highest category and includes a master bedroom, separate living room, dining area, and dedicated workspace. Premium furnishings, curated art, and enhanced privacy controls are standard in this suite class.

Bathroom facilities include dual vanity, soaking tub, and separate shower compartment with luxury amenities. The suite supports private in-room check-in, subject to staffing schedule. Security and access protocols are stricter for this category, and event hosting within the suite requires prior management approval.
