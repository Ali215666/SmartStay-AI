Breakfast is served daily from 7:00 AM to 10:30 AM in the main restaurant. Menu includes continental, local hot dishes, bakery items, and rotating seasonal selections. Takeaway breakfast boxes are available for early departures with prior notice.

Guests with breakfast-included rates must present room number verification at entry. External visitors may dine at published walk-in rates subject to seating capacity. Last seating is 20 minutes before close to ensure service completion and sanitation turnover before lunch preparation.
