Emergency exits are posted on the back of each guestroom door and in corridor wayfinding signage. In case of alarm, guests must use stairwells and avoid elevators. Assembly points are marked outside the main entrance and annex parking court.

For medical emergencies, dial the emergency extension from room phone for immediate response and ambulance coordination. Fire extinguishers, smoke detectors, and first-aid kits are inspected on scheduled intervals. Security and engineering teams conduct regular drills to maintain readiness and response accuracy.
